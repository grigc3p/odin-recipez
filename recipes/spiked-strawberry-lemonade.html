A ready cocktail with a lemonade and spirit taste

The following is a recipe for a strawberry lemonade drink spiked with vodka. It is a refreshing drink to both cool you down during these hot summer days and also pysche you up.

Ingridients

  • 1 cup sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 cups water, divided
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste (optional)
  • 1 cup vodka, divided
  • ice, as needed

Directions

  1. Combine strawberries, lemon juice, and 1 cup water in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a pitcher and add remaining 2 cups water. Taste; add sugar as desired.
  3. Fill each glass with ice, add 2 ounces vodka per glass, and top off with strawberry lemonade.
